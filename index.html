Biography

I am an M.Phil. student under the supervision of Dr. Heming Cui and Prof. Cho-Li Wang. My research interests span across Artificial Intelligence, Big-data System and Software Testing. My research outcomes include five papers published in international conferences（e.g., DSN, ATC, SRDS), and two patents transferred to Huawei (both patents were awarded “High Potential Value Patent” title by Huawei)。
