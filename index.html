Biography

I am a PhD student at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology under the supervision of Prof. Shing-Chi Cheung. I was an M.Phil. student at the University of Hong Kong under the supervision of Dr. Heming Cui and Prof. Cho-Li Wang. My research lies at the intersection of Software Engineering and Artificial Intelligence, currently focusing on exposing software faults or vulnerabilities using Large Language Model.
